Mohamed Iqbal Digital Profile

MOHAMED IQBAL BIN MOHAMED ALI MARICAR (aka MOIQBAL)

Has over 40 over years experience in the wholesale business and over 11 years in IT Training. He is a practicing ecommerce businessman, and his current interest and hobby is to share his knowledge to entrepreneurs, small businesses, self employed and to whoever have dreams and visions for success 

He is presently training IT skills to adults, especially the 'baby boomers' in particular. He sees a need to help them equip with IT knowledge so that they can enjoy the next phase of their lives with new opportunities to further their knowledge, learning new things, enjoyments, entertainments, socialise more and not be left out in society.

He thus concentrate in empowering adults in basic IT such as, Microsoft Office, Basic Internet Marketing, and Web Developing with Word-Press, Ecommerce Website, Digital Marketing and also Smart phones and Tablets. Mohamed Iqbal (MOIQBAL) holds WSQ Diploma in Interactive Media and  WSQ Full Advanced Certificate in Training and Assessment (ACTA) WSQ Diploma in Adult Continuing Education (DACE) with the Institute of Adult Education. LCCI Sales and Marketing certificates, WSQ-SIRS Digital Professional Course for Online Retail Business (ECOMMERCE), and WSQ-SIRS Social media marketing & Search Engine Optimization.
